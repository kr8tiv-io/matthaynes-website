# Matt Haynes Personal Site Product Context

## Register
brand

## Product Purpose
Matthaynes.fun is Matt Haynes' personal digital atelier: a premium founder/operator portfolio, story surface, and operating links library. The site should make Matt feel sharp, cinematic, taste-led, technically current, and unusually capable across AI systems, brand/growth execution, venture work, and public building.

## Audience
- Founders, operators, clients, collaborators, partners, interviewers, and investors evaluating Matt quickly.
- Designers and technical builders who will judge the site by taste, motion quality, and implementation craft.
- People arriving from X, GitHub, Linktree, KR8TiV AI, KR8TiV Agency, Jarvis, KIN, and the Aura ecosystem.

## Success Criteria
- The first viewport feels exceptional and personal, with Matt Haynes as the clear brand signal.
- The background video transitions remain intact and become more authored.
- The old fog/cloud/globby shader, firefly/rain clutter, tropical flora, and default glass-card aesthetic are removed.
- The site feels editorial, haute couture, cinematic, and systems-minded without copying kr8tiv.io.
- The links route remains practical, searchable, mobile-friendly, and fast.
- Static Hostinger deployment stays simple and route-compatible.

## Brand Tone
Refined, direct, slightly dangerous, intelligent, builder-native, and human. The writing should feel authored by a founder with taste and scars, not a generic personal-brand landing page.

## Anti-References
- AI-generated glassmorphism cards.
- Tropical fog/cloud/flora decoration.
- Same-sized repeated card grids.
- Gradient text.
- Purple neon SaaS darkness.
- Founder-page filler copy.
- Hero metric templates.
- Linktree clones.

## Strategic Principles
- Video is the cinematic layer; shaders should refract or choreograph it, not cover it.
- Typography carries the identity before decoration does.
- One memorable interaction beats many small gimmicks.
- Motion must be purposeful, scroll-aware, and reduced-motion safe.
- The site is a brand surface first and a utility surface second.
